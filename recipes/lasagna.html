Description

A hearty baked pasta dish layered with beef, ham, creamy béchamel, napolitana sauce and melted cheese — comfort food at its richest.

$48 LG, $25 SM


Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • beef
  • ham
  • béchamel
  • napolitana sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until al dente.
  2. Prepare beef and ham filling with napolitana sauce.
  3. Layer pasta, meat, ham, béchamel and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake until golden and bubbling.
