Description

Rolled pasta parcels stuffed with a creamy blend of spinach, roasted pumpkin, ricotta, parmesan and feta, baked in a classic napolitana sauce.

$48 LG, $25 SM


Ingredients

  • Rolled pasta
  • oasted pumpkin
  • ricotta
  • mint.
  • feta
  • parmesan
  • napolitana sauce

Steps

  1. Roast pumpkin until tender.
  2. Mix with spinach, ricotta, parmesan and feta.
  3. Fill rolled pasta sheets with the mixture.
  4. Place in a baking dish, cover with napolitana sauce and bake until golden.
