Description

A cross-cultural take on meatloaf combining marinated minced meat with bulgar wheat, fresh parsley and mint, drawing on the bold flavours of both Brazilian and Lebanese cuisine.

$44


Ingredients

  • Marinated mince
  • bulgar wheat
  • fresh parsley
  • mint.

Steps

  1. Marinate minced meat with herbs and seasoning.
  2. Mix in bulgar wheat, fresh parsley and mint.
  3. Shape into a loaf and bake until cooked through.
